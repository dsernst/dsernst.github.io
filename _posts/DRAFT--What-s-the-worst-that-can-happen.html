I've found this useful and you may too.


I've been experimenting recently with a new method for pushing past the resistance and getting off my ass. Whenever I am feel doubt or a lack of initiative, I can ask myself: "What's the worst that can happen?"


This single line has helped me make so much more shit happen. 


And the even better part of this method is that the things that I get done this way in retrospect often seem so much more valuable than your typical activities directed from auto-pilot.


I'm certainly not an expert, but it seems to be really helping me and hopefully this can help you too.


So try it out! What's the worst that can happen?
