Have you tried WorkFlowy.com yet? I use it to track to-do lists, current projects, class and meeting notes, shopping lists, journaling, etc. It's like the ultimate notebook: you can use it for everything and then some. I've never used anything better.

Everything is automatically saved every 10 seconds and backed-up. You can invite other people to work with you.

It's very easy to sign up, and make sure you watch the quick How-To videos. 

Using this link <https://workflowy.com/?ref=9137aa4> will give both of us 50% extra space for free.
